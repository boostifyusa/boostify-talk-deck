Stay safe online.

The same tools that grow your business get targeted. A few free habits protect you.

Boostify · Get Seen, Get Hired16

The scams hitting businesses right now.

Imposter scams were the #1 reported fraud in 2025: $3.5 billion lost, up nearly 20% (FTC, June 2026). A fake email from a “vendor” or “boss” that redirects a payment (business email compromise) cost $3.05 billion (FBI, 2025). Fake invoices for tech support, domains, or SEO target small businesses.

AI cuts both ways. Scammers now clone voices and write convincing emails. $893 million was lost to AI-enabled fraud in 2025 (FBI). Treat any urgent payment request as suspicious.

From billing@geek-squad-secure.com
Subject Invoice #99480 — Auto-renewal $499.99
Your annual support plan has renewed. To cancel and get a refund, call this number now and confirm your bank details.

Pay to: new account ending 8841
✕ Sender domain is not the real company.
✕ Urgency and a refund hook.
✕ New payment account you have never used.
FTC, June 2026 · FBI / IC3 2025 ReportStay safe · 17

Protect yourself today. It’s free.

Verify before you pay.
Call a known number, not the one printed on the invoice or in the email.
Decide who can approve a payment, and write it down.
One simple rule stops most fake-invoice and wire scams.
Turn on two-factor (or passkeys) for email, Google, and social.
It is the single biggest lock on your accounts.
Use a password manager. One strong password per account.
Reused passwords are how one leak becomes ten break-ins.
Lock down your site: SSL on, backups running.
So a bad day is an inconvenience, not a disaster.

4. Let AI do the busywork.

58% of small businesses already use AI, and the top use is marketing and content (U.S. Chamber, 2025). It will not run your business. It saves you time on the words.

Draft a week of posts. Turn a job into a caption. Write the first draft of a web page. Reply to reviews. Answer common questions after hours.

Three rules before you trust it

1. Check it, it can be confidently wrong.   2. Keep your voice.   3. Never paste private or customer data.

PROMPT“Write 3 Instagram captions for a finished driveway job in Clovis.”
1. Cracked and crumbling to smooth and solid in 2 days. This Clovis driveway is ready for another 20 years.
2. New driveway, who dis? Swipe for the before. Booking estimates across the Valley.
3. Quality concrete, done right the first time. Proud of this one, Clovis. Tag a neighbor who needs it.

AI that answers when you can’t.

Most calls and messages land after hours, and the first business to reply usually wins the job. An AI agent can answer, qualify, and book, so you stop losing work while you sleep.
